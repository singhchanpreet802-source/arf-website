ARF PROJECTS & INITIATIVES

Building
Impact.

From urban research to real-world implementation, ARF develops projects that aim to solve practical challenges through evidence-based action.

Current Projects

Why Does Bareilly Choke?

Researching traffic congestion, mobility bottlenecks, road design and signal efficiency in Bareilly.

Why Does Bareilly Flood?

Studying drainage systems, flood-prone zones and urban water management challenges.

Why Does Construction Never End?

Analysing prolonged road construction failure on the Prem Nagar–Model Town corridor through field evidence and systemic analysis.

Municipal Partnership — Bareilly

Translating ARF's three case studies into coordinated implementation with Bareilly's municipal agencies through the ARF-CIF framework: one committee, one pilot, one tool.
