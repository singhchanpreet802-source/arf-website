ABOUT ANEJA RESEARCH FOUNDATION

Research.
With Purpose.

Aneja Research Foundation (ARF) is a student-led research, implementation and innovation organization established in 2026. We believe that research should not end with reports. It should lead to action, implementation and measurable impact.

Our Mission

To identify real-world problems, conduct evidence-based research, and implement practical solutions that create measurable impact.

Our Vision

To become India's leading student-led research and implementation foundation, empowering young people to solve real-world challenges.
